# Others

* [WeevilEye](https://www.sparkfun.com/sparkfun-weevileye-beginner-soldering-kit.html): bug soldering kit where "eyes" light up in response to a photosensor 
* [Blinkyparts Unicorn](https://github.com/blinkyparts/unicorn): light up a unicorn through a PCB (simple schematic, cool KiCad design)
* [555 Aeroplane](https://github.com/ANG13T/555-plane-pcb): dual LED circuit with timer chip, can be redesigned in your own form (hexpansion?)
* [HakkaaTachi](https://github.com/hakkaatachi/hakkaa-hardware): Persistence of Vision (POV) kit intro to surface mount soldering and microcontroller programming with Embedded Rust
* [EMF Tildagon Badge](https://tildagon.badge.emfcamp.org/): hexagon badge slots you can plug your own hexpansion designs into
* [EMF Tildagon OG Hexpansion](https://github.com/emfcamp/badge-2024-hardware/tree/main/hexpansion)
* [EMF Tildagon Goosepansion](https://github.com/skyler84-tildagon-addons/goosepansion)
* [EMF Explorer Badge](https://darcyneal.com/emf-explorer-badge/): listen to the world of hidden electromagnetic frequencies around you
* [RP2040-based butterfly-shaped wearable](https://github.com/Alexandra182/butterfly-OHS26-workshop) with NeoPixel LEDs and a Qwiic connector/IR transceiver - great example of a fully assembled board
